Luis Jiménez Navajas

aQuantum, Alarcos Research Group

About

PhD student in Advanced Computer Technologies and adjunct professor in the Faculty of Social Sciences and IT in the University of Castilla-La Mancha. Current member of the scientific research group aQuantum, working on the software modernization of classical-quantum information systems with specific focus on software reverse engineering.
